Definition of Authorization

Authorization is having an official permission for something to happen, or the act of giving someone official permission to do something.

Click here for the Reference

With Authorization vs With No Authorization

With Authorization, everyone can access information and see the content but it will be less secure because you're giving access in public. With No Authorization, people can't access the information and see the content but it's secure and you can just give private access to designated audience.
